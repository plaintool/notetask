Changelly

You can easily send cryptocurrency to a wallet by paying in regular currency through the suggested website.

Just follow the link, specify how much you want to send, choose the cryptocurrency, and select your preferred payment method such as GPay, PayPal, Visa, etc.

On the next step, simply copy the wallet address of the selected cryptocurrency (BTC by default) and paste it into the field.

On the next step, you will be taken to a page to pay with your chosen method in regular currency.

Follow the instructions on the payment page; it should be straightforward.
